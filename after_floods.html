Reentering Your
Flooded Home

When you first reenter your home:

try to return to your home during the daytime so that you do not have to use any lights. Use battery-powered flashlights and lanterns, rather than candles, gas lanterns, or torches.

Use safe water.

Floodwater can contaminate your drinking water. Some contaminants from surface water get into the groundwater and affect private drinking water wells and municipal water systems that use groundwater.

Use generators and other electrical equipment safely.

Talk to your utility company about using electrical equipment, including power generators.
All electrical equipment and appliances must be completely dry before returning them to service.

Clean up your home safely.

Take steps to protect yourself and your loved ones during your cleanup after a flood.
