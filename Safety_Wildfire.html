Reduce your smoke exposure by wearing a respirator

A respirator is a mask that fits tightly to your face to filter out smoke before you breathe it in.
If you have heart or lung disease ask your doctor if it is safe to wear a respirator.
Avoid using candles, gas, fireplaces, or aerosol sprays.

Pay attention to any health symptoms

if you have asthma, COPD, heart disease, or are pregnant. Get medical help if you need it.
